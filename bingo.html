RIP Tide Bingo!

How to:

  1. A bingo card can only be used for one stream - you can't continue a previous card for a new stream
  2. Any RIP Tide streams count
  3. Click a square as you hear or see the square on stream to highlight it
  4. If you accidentally click the wrong square, you can click it again to un-highlight it
  5. To win, screenshot your bingo card and send it to a member of RIP Tide
